Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

Q: Do you offer custom cakes and pastries?

A: Yes! We specialize in custom cakes and pastries for all occasions. Please reach out for more information or to place an order.

Q: Can I place an order online?

A: Currently, we accept online inquiries and orders for pickup. For delivery options, please contact us directly.

Q: Do you have gluten-free and vegan options?

A: We offer a selection of gluten-free and vegan items. Contact us for a full list of options!

Q: What is the ordering process for custom cakes?

A: Simply contact us with your cake design ideas and details. We’ll work with you to create the perfect cake for your event!

Q: How far in advance should I place my order?

A: We recommend placing custom orders at least one week in advance. For larger or highly customized cakes, two weeks is ideal.

Q: Can I make changes to my order after placing it?

A: Yes, you can make changes up to 48 hours before your pickup or delivery date. Please contact us as soon as possible.

Q :What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept cash, credit cards, and mobile payments. For custom orders, a deposit may be required.

Q: Are your products allergy-friendly?

A: We offer some gluten-free and vegan options, but please note that our kitchen handles allergens like nuts, dairy, and gluten.

Q: Can I pick up my order on the same day?

A: Yes, for select items we offer same-day pickup. Custom orders may require more time, so please inquire in advance.

Q: What is your refund policy?

A: For standard items, we offer refunds or exchanges if the product is unsatisfactory. Custom orders are non-refundable.
